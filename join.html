RESEARCH PATHS

Choose one of three directions.

01 / EXPERIMENT

Experimental research

Study infection and resistance genes, protein function, candidate activity and synthetic biology at the bench.

  • Cloning and knockout
  • Strain construction
  • Expression, purification and phenotype
02 / SIMULATION

Molecular simulation

Understand proteins, mutations and candidate compounds through structure, motion and interaction.

  • Dynamics simulation
  • Docking and virtual screening
  • Trajectory and free energy
